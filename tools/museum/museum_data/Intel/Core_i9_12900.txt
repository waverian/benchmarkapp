Historical note:
Alder Lake is a next-gen Intel architecture. Finally moved to 10 nm, and it is *much* much faster than anything before it.
Dropped AVX512, but gained "Efficiency cores" (aka small cores), plus a much better branch prediction.
This is the 1st gen to Introduce DDR5 memory.
